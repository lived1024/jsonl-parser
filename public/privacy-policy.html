Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 26, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to JSONL Parser ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • JSON/JSONL data you input into our parser (stored locally in your browser only)
  • Learning progress and preferences (stored locally in your browser)
  • Language preferences

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage analytics through Google Analytics (anonymized)
  • Browser type, device information, and IP address
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referral sources

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our JSON parsing services
  • To improve user experience and website functionality
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize performance
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your JSON data and learning progress are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

4.2 Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized usage statistics. This data is processed according to Google's privacy policy.

4.3 Security Measures

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • No server-side storage of user data
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual visitors.

5.2 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze site usage through Google Analytics
  • Serve relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: You can access your locally stored data through browser developer tools
  • Deletion: You can clear your data by clearing browser storage or using our clear data function
  • Opt-out: You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking and personalized ads
  • Cookie Control: You can manage cookies through your browser settings

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this privacy policy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Data Retention

  • Local browser data: Retained until you clear it or uninstall your browser
  • Analytics data: Retained according to Google Analytics retention settings (26 months by default)
  • Server logs: Retained for 30 days for security and performance monitoring

12. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, our legal basis for processing personal information includes:

  • Consent: For analytics and advertising cookies
  • Legitimate Interest: For website functionality and security
  • Performance of Contract: For providing our services

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:
